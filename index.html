Top 5 College Survival Tips 📚

1. Create a Digital Calendar: Sync your class schedule, assignments, and club activities to stay on track.

2. Learn Time Blocking: Allocate specific hours for studying, fun, and rest. You'll thank yourself later.

3. Join a Club: Whether it’s coding, arts, or entrepreneurship—networking starts here.

4. Build a Portfolio: Use GitHub or a personal website to show off your mini-projects (even if they’re simple!).

5. Use Campus Resources: Attend seminars, use the library, and don’t skip internship orientation sessions.
